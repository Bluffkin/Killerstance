We are happy to announce that the new song and single from the up and coming album is in it's mastering stages and should be released in the next two weeks! Certainly this year! Oblivion has been reviewed by a proffesional sound engineer and has given us the thumbs up to let us know it's sounding great! We hope you guys think so too! It's nearly here people...just that little bit longer! Make sure to follow our SoundCloud to keep up-to-date on the release of the single, and of course our ALBUM!!
